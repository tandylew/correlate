Bessent Squawk Box
6–9am ET  |  CNBC
r ≈ −0.95 at open
Bessent appeared on CNBC Squawk Box from Paris confirming the US was allowing Iranian oil tankers through Hormuz. WTI fell from $100+ in pre-market. USO gapped down $4.33 (−3.6%) from Friday close. SPY futures +0.87%. The negative correlation was locked in before the opening bell.
WHY IT MATTERS
Pure supply-restoration signal with no ambiguity. Bessent gave two clean anchor points: Iranian exports are allowed through, and Treasury is not distorting futures markets. SPY up, USO down — textbook inverse correlation.
WSJ: Naval Coalition Report
~10:45am ET  |  Most significant
FLIPS to ~+0.50
A WSJ report hit at ~10:45am that the US was about to announce a formal naval coalition to escort tankers through Hormuz. For ~45 minutes, BOTH SPY AND USO rose simultaneously — the session's only window of positive correlation.
WHY IT MATTERS
Classic dual-read ambiguity. "Naval coalition to escort tankers" has two contradictory interpretations: (1) strait reopens safely — bullish equities, bearish oil; OR (2) military buildup means Iran resists — bullish oil. Both equity and commodity desks bid simultaneously, collapsing correlation to near-zero and briefly pushing it positive.
Trump: "Less Than Enthusiastic"
~12:30pm ET  |  White House
Snaps back to −0.90
Trump told reporters at ~12:30pm that the coalition wasn't assembled yet: "Some are less than enthusiastic." He also issued fresh Iran warnings. USO reversed from $112.90 to $116.80 (+3.4% in 45 min). SPY fell ~$2.50 from highs.
WHY IT MATTERS
Tail-risk reintroduction. The WSJ story had begun pricing in clean de-escalation; Trump's hedging reinstated the contested-Strait scenario. The speed of the reversal (r from ~0 to −0.9 in under an hour) shows the market has developed a conditioned reflex for this inverse trade.
Nvidia GTC Keynote
2pm ET  |  Jensen Huang
Stabilizes at −0.78
Jensen Huang announced $1 trillion in projected Blackwell/Vera Rubin orders through 2027. NVDA +4%, touching $188. SPY hit session high $672.07. USO drifted to close $114.86. Correlation held negative but mechanism changed.
WHY IT MATTERS
GTC introduced an independent positive SPY catalyst with zero relationship to Hormuz. The afternoon's negative correlation was driven by tech demand pulling SPY higher while oil fell on Bessent's morning framing — two different causal stories producing the same correlation sign.
